Chicken Soup

Description

Originally from allrecipes.com, by user kaylahess. Named “Lemony Cream of Chicken Soup”.

kaylahess described it as “This recipe makes a big pot of yummy lemon chicken soup! It's super creamy and the chicken and rice adds a great texture! This makes a large pot, you might want to try cutting it in half the first time you make it.”

Ingredients

  • 2 (48 ounce) containers chicken broth
  • 4 (12 ounce) cans chicken chunks, drained
  • 1 ½ cups white rice
  • 2 (26 ounce) cans cream of chicken soup
  • ¾ cup lemon juice, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine chicken broth and canned chicken in a pot; bring to a boil. Add rice; simmer until rice is tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Stir in cream of chicken soup and lemon juice. Cook, stirring occasionally, until soup is hot, 5 to 10 more minutes.
